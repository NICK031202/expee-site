Privacy Policy

Expee · Last updated 12 June 2026

Expee is an expense tracker. This page explains exactly what data we handle, why, and what control you have over it.

What we collect

  • Phone number — your account identity and sign-in (one-time codes). We don't require your name or email; a display name is optional.
  • Financial entries you create — expenses, income, categories, budgets, and notes you add.
  • Receipts and bank statements you upload — the photo or PDF, and the entries extracted from it.

How your documents are processed

Receipt photos and statement PDFs are read by an AI service (Google Gemini) solely to extract the transaction details — vendor, date, amount, line items. They are never used to train models and never sold or shared for advertising. There is no ad tracking of any kind in Expee.

Where data lives

Your data is stored in our database (hosted on Render) and is visible to your account alone. We are building end-to-end encryption so that stored financial data becomes unreadable to anyone but you, including us.

Deleting your data

In the app: Settings → Delete all data permanently removes every expense, income entry, imported statement, and uploaded document. For full account deletion, contact us and we remove the account and everything attached within 30 days.

Third parties we rely on

  • Google Gemini — document text extraction at processing time
  • Render — application and database hosting
  • Apple — sign-in codes are delivered as SMS; payments (when available) are handled by the App Store
